What's Intelligent Diagnosis?

This is a model created to ease the lives of both patients and doctors. Those who stay at home and end up getting sick are prone to be stuck in a dilemma: should I take my chances staying at home or going to the hospital? In a time like this, doctors are essential in taking quick and virtual action. Unfortunately, that generally isn't the case with the lengthy times required for each diagnosis. Our model revolves around the idea of having an AI bot diagnoBot asking all the right questions to patients and summarizing said data into a form of a report. This report is then sent to the doctor who can quickly diagnosis the patient along with any additional notes. A fast yet effective method to keep yourself safe from the comfort of your home.

How do I Navigate the Site?

You can start off by registering an account as either a patient or a doctor. Then, go to your respective tabs and select what action you would like to perform: for a patient, you could fill in prediagnosis details using diagnoBot, while doctors can look into their dashboards for received reports from patients.

What are the Upcoming Updates to the Site?

We are eventually looking forward to creating predictive analysis models that offer suggestions to doctors based off previous records of a patient. This key insight would be extremely useful in speeding up the doctor's diagnosis process. We are also looking towards increasing diagnoBot's dataset and making it more versatile when handling patients.
